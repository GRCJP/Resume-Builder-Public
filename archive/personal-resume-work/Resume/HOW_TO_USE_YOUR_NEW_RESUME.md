## Interview Preparation

When you get interview requests, prepare using your resume:

### For Each Bullet, Have a STAR Story:
- **Situation:** What was the challenge?
- **Task:** What needed to be done?
- **Action:** What did you specifically do?
- **Result:** What was the measurable outcome?

### Example:
**Bullet:** "Reduced vulnerabilities from 70,000 to 12,000"

**STAR Story:**
- **S:** Inherited a vulnerability backlog of 70,000 findings across 10,000+ assets with no prioritization
- **T:** Needed to reduce risk while not overwhelming engineering teams
- **A:** Built automated triage using CVSS, EPSS, and asset criticality; created routing workflows; implemented SLA tracking
- **Result:** Reduced to 12,000 in 18 months (83% reduction), improved MTTR by 40%, zero critical findings in last 3 audits

### Common GRC Interview Questions:

1. **"Walk me through your RMF experience"**
   - Use your ISSO role bullets
   - Mention specific agencies and frameworks
   - Show full lifecycle understanding

2. **"How do you prioritize vulnerabilities?"**
   - Explain your risk-based approach
   - Mention CVSS, EPSS, asset criticality
   - Give specific example

3. **"Describe your automation experience"**
   - Use Featured Projects section
   - Explain AWS Config ‚Üí Jira workflow
   - Show business value (70% time savings)

4. **"How do you handle audit findings?"**
   - Explain POA&M management
   - Show remediation tracking
   - Mention zero material weaknesses

5. **"Tell me about a time you had to explain technical risk to executives"**
   - Use cross-functional collaboration examples
   - Show translation ability
   - Emphasize business impact
