# Resume Tailor Feature Guide

## What It Does

The Resume Tailor automatically generates a customized version of your resume for each specific job, pulling from your actual experience to highlight relevant skills.

---

## How It Works

### **Step 1: Upload & Analyze**
1. Upload your master resume
2. Paste a job description
3. Click "Analyze Job Match"
4. See your match score and missing keywords

### **Step 2: Tailor Resume**
1. After analysis, a new "Resume Tailor" section appears
2. Click "Tailor Resume" button
3. Tool generates customized version automatically

### **Step 3: Review & Download**
1. Review the tailored version
2. See estimated score improvement
3. Download for submission
4. Make final adjustments if needed

## Troubleshooting

### **"Enhancements don't match my experience"**
- Review your master resume
- Add missing experience there first
- Re-upload and try again

### **"Score didn't improve much"**
- Check if missing keywords are in your background
- If not, consider if role is right fit
- Focus on jobs that match your skills

### **"Too much text added"**
- Edit the tailored version
- Keep only most relevant enhancements
- Condense to fit 2 pages

### **"Want to add more"**
- Edit the downloaded file
- Add job-specific examples
- Customize further manually
