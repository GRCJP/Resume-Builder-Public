## Color Palette

### **Backgrounds:**
- Main: `bg-gradient-to-br from-slate-900 via-purple-900 to-slate-900`
- Cards: `bg-slate-800/50 backdrop-blur-sm`
- Headers: `bg-gradient-to-r from-purple-900/50 to-pink-900/50`

### **Borders:**
- Primary: `border-purple-500/30`
- Hover: `border-purple-400`
- Active: `border-purple-500`

### **Text:**
- Headings: `text-purple-100` to `text-purple-200`
- Body: `text-purple-300` to `text-purple-400`
- Muted: `text-purple-400` to `text-purple-500`

### **Buttons:**
- Primary: `bg-gradient-to-r from-purple-600 to-pink-600`
- Hover: `hover:from-purple-700 hover:to-pink-700`
- Shadow: `shadow-lg` with purple glow
