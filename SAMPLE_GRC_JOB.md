## Sample 1: GRC Analyst

**Company:** TechCorp Financial Services  
**Location:** Remote  
**Experience:** 5-8 years

### Job Description

We are seeking a GRC Analyst to join our Information Security team. The ideal candidate will have strong experience in governance, risk management, and compliance frameworks.

**Responsibilities:**
- Conduct risk assessments and maintain enterprise risk register
- Perform control testing and validation for SOX, PCI DSS, and ISO 27001 compliance
- Support internal and external audits by coordinating evidence collection
- Develop and maintain security policies and procedures aligned with regulatory requirements
- Manage vendor risk assessment program for third-party service providers
- Create executive-level risk reports and dashboards using metrics and KPIs
- Collaborate with IT, Legal, and Compliance teams on governance initiatives
- Monitor compliance with security controls and identify gaps
- Track remediation activities and ensure timely closure of audit findings

**Required Qualifications:**
- 5+ years of experience in GRC, information security, or IT audit
- Strong knowledge of NIST CSF, ISO 27001, SOX, and PCI DSS frameworks
- Experience with GRC platforms such as Archer, ServiceNow, or RSAM
- Proven ability to conduct risk assessments and control testing
- Excellent documentation and communication skills
- Bachelor's degree in Information Security, Computer Science, or related field

**Preferred Qualifications:**
- CISSP, CISA, CISM, or CRISC certification
- Experience with HIPAA or FedRAMP compliance
- Knowledge of Python or SQL for automation and reporting
- Experience in financial services or regulated industry

## Sample 2: Senior GRC Engineer

**Company:** CloudSecure Inc.  
**Location:** Hybrid - San Francisco, CA  
**Experience:** 7-10 years

### Job Description

CloudSecure is looking for a Senior GRC Engineer to lead our compliance and risk management programs as we scale our SaaS platform.

**What You'll Do:**
- Lead implementation and maintenance of security compliance frameworks (SOC 2, ISO 27001, FedRAMP)
- Design and execute risk assessment methodology across cloud infrastructure
- Develop automated compliance monitoring and reporting solutions using Python and APIs
- Manage relationships with external auditors and coordinate audit activities
- Build and maintain GRC tooling using ServiceNow or similar platforms
- Create security awareness training programs for employees
- Establish metrics and KPIs to measure security posture and compliance status
- Perform gap analysis and develop remediation roadmaps
- Lead incident response planning and business continuity exercises
- Mentor junior GRC team members

**Must Have:**
- 7+ years in GRC, security engineering, or compliance roles
- Deep expertise in SOC 2, ISO 27001, and cloud security frameworks
- Hands-on experience with GRC platforms (ServiceNow GRC, Vanta, Drata, or SecureFrame)
- Strong technical skills including scripting (Python, PowerShell) and API integration
- Experience with AWS, Azure, or GCP security controls
- Proven track record managing audit processes from planning through remediation
- Excellent stakeholder management and executive communication skills

**Nice to Have:**
- CISSP, CCSP, or CISM certification
- FedRAMP or StateRAMP experience
- Experience with SIEM tools (Splunk, ELK) for compliance monitoring
- Background in DevSecOps or security automation
- Master's degree in Cybersecurity or related field

## Sample 3: GRC Program Manager

**Company:** HealthTech Solutions  
**Location:** Boston, MA  
**Experience:** 10+ years

### Job Description

HealthTech Solutions seeks an experienced GRC Program Manager to oversee our enterprise risk and compliance programs in the healthcare technology sector.

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Establish and mature enterprise GRC program aligned with HIPAA, HITRUST, and SOC 2
- Lead cross-functional teams in risk assessment and mitigation activities
- Manage vendor risk management program for 100+ third-party vendors
- Oversee internal audit function and coordinate external regulatory examinations
- Develop executive-level risk reporting and board presentations
- Implement GRC technology platform (Archer, OnSpring, or similar)
- Define and track compliance KPIs and risk metrics
- Lead policy governance committee and annual policy review process
- Manage budget and resources for GRC team of 5-7 professionals
- Ensure alignment between security controls and business objectives
- Drive continuous improvement in control effectiveness and efficiency

**Required Experience:**
- 10+ years in GRC, compliance, or risk management roles
- 5+ years in healthcare or regulated industry
- Expert knowledge of HIPAA, HITRUST, SOC 2, and ISO 27001
- Proven experience implementing and managing GRC platforms
- Strong project management and program leadership skills
- Experience managing teams and developing talent
- Demonstrated ability to influence senior leadership

**Required Certifications:**
- CISSP, CISA, CISM, or CRISC (at least one required)
- HITRUST CSF Practitioner (preferred)

**Technical Skills:**
- GRC platforms (Archer RSA, ServiceNow GRC, OnSpring, RSAM)
- Risk assessment methodologies and frameworks
- Data analysis and visualization (Excel, Tableau, Power BI)
- Understanding of cloud security (AWS, Azure)
- Familiarity with SIEM and security monitoring tools

## How to Use These Samples

1. **Copy the entire job description** (from "Job Description" through the end)
2. **Paste into the Job Description Analyzer** in the GRC Resume Builder
3. **Click "Analyze Job Match"** to see:
   - Your match score
   - Keywords you're missing
   - Tailored suggestions for improvement
4. **Update your resume** based on the recommendations
5. **Re-analyze** to see your improved score

## What to Look For

- **Match Score:** Aim for 70%+ for roles you're qualified for
- **Missing Keywords:** These are opportunities to add relevant experience
- **Suggestions:** Written in natural language to help you reframe your experience
- **Certifications:** Note which certs are "required" vs "preferred"

Remember: The goal isn't to fake experience, but to present your actual experience using the language and frameworks that GRC roles require.
